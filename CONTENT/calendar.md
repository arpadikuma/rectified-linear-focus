# Content Calendar

**Goal:** 1 technical post + 1 progress update per week  
**Platforms:** LinkedIn + Twitter (primary), Medium/blog (secondary)

---

## Weekly Format

### Technical Post (Tuesday)
- Deep dive on one aspect of what you're building
- 300-500 words
- Links to relevant projects/code
- Teaches something specific

### Progress Post (Friday)
- What shipped this week
- What didn't work
- What's next
- Honest, raw tone
- Link to this roadmap repo

## Distribution Strategy

### LinkedIn
- Post Tuesday morning (8-9am your timezone)
- Post Friday morning
- Use 3-4 relevant hashtags
- Tag projects/tools mentioned
- Link to full post or repo

### Twitter
- Thread format on Tuesday (expand the technical post)
- Shorter progress update Friday
- Quote/engage with creator community
- Ask questions to your audience

### Optional: Blog/Medium
- Month 2: Publish 2-3 posts on Medium or personal blog
- Allows for longer-form content
- Better SEO than LinkedIn

---

## Metrics to Track

- LinkedIn: Impressions, clicks, comments
- Twitter: Retweets, likes, replies
- Email (if you build a list): Subscribers
- Real metric: Clicks to repo = interest

---

## Links to Include in Every Post

- 🔗 **This roadmap:** [Your repo link]
- 🔗 **Current project:** [Specific project folder]
- 🔗 **Full details:** Link to detailed project README

---

## Voice Guide

- **Authentic:** Share what's real, not polished
- **Specific:** Use examples, numbers, concrete details
- **Honest:** Mention what didn't work too
- **Helpful:** Share lessons others can apply
- **Focused:** One main idea per post

---

## Notes

- Don't wait for perfection to post
- Raw feedback is more interesting than success
- Engage with comments (algorithm + community)
- Keep consistency > perfection
